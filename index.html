About

FLo-Talk started with a bus ride from Tallahassee to Miami. This diverse state is full of cities, fields, towns, smaller towns, and even smaller towns. FLo-Talk gives you the chance to show your slice of the sunshine state to all Floridians, from Miami-Dade to Escambia County. One lucky user from each county will be selected daily, and that user will be able to post a picture and caption. No one else can post in that county, only comment. The point is that YOU have the mic and are able to guide the discussion for one day.
